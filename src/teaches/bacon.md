# Bacon

### Overview

- [ ] Crispy climbing game
- [ ] Objective: first to 6/8/10/24 VP or 8/6 rounds (4p/6p)
- [ ] Play combos/specials to go out; 1st place enables team scoring

### Flow

- [ ] Round start: deal all breakfast cards evenly + 2 Bacon cards to each player
- [ ] Lead: play any combo or special
- [ ] Follow: beat previous play or pass
  - [ ] Beat combo with higher rank of same type OR any special
  - [ ] Beat special with higher rank of same special OR any "better" special (further down menu)
- [ ] Passing: can re-enter trick later
- [ ] Trick Win: discard played cards; winner leads next or chooses teammate to lead (no table talk)
  - [ ] Empty Hand: if team goes out on winning play, lead passes to first player on left with cards

### Combos & Specials

- [ ] Max 6 cards per play
- [ ] Must maintain type: cannot follow a combo with a different combo (e.g., pair followed by trip)
- [ ] Specials only: once a special is played, only specials can beat it until the trick ends
- [ ] Bacon: wild rank/suit; rank can be deck max + 1; must state rank/suit when played

### Round End & Scoring

- [ ] Going out: claim next available score token (1, 2, 3...)
- [ ] End trigger: only one player has cards remaining
- [ ] Individual Scoring (3p/5p): score VP listed on your token
- [ ] Team Scoring (4p/6p): only the team with the 1st place player scores
  - [ ] Team earns VP for every teammate token listed on the 1st place "Score" menu (e.g., 1st place earns points for 3rd and 5th)
