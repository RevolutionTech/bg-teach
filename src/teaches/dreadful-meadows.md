# Dreadful Meadows

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe: farming candy
  - [ ] Each player plays a different Confectioner with a different special power
- [ ] Objective: most VPs (placement of harvesters, completing Concoction cards, leftover candy)
- [ ] Game end: final round triggered when 1 harvester remaining, bag is empty, or all Concoction cards drawn
- [ ] Different candies have different values, they are used for payment and you can't get change
- [ ] Turn overview: take an action
  - [ ] Can fulfill Concoction cards anytime during turn

### Actions

- [ ] Purchase patch: spend candy from your patches to buy and place patches from the market
  - [ ] Cost: patch value + market position
  - [ ] After placement, market refills
  - [ ] Cannot change patch position after placement
- [ ] Place sugar sprite: place on empty patch to generate candy on all empty adjacent patches
  - [ ] May discard candy before placing
- [ ] Purchase harvester: spend candy from your patches to buy and place a harvester on a patch surrounded by 4 other patches
  - [ ] Cost: sum of surrounding patch values
  - [ ] May retrieve sugar sprite from space (for no bonus) before placing
  - [ ] Cannot place on Dreadful Tree patch or patch with a harvester
  - [ ] Cannot move harvesters once placed
  - [ ] In addition to scoring points, harvesters chain the candy generation when placing a sugar sprite
- [ ] Retrieve sugar sprites: trigger bonuses depending on the patch
  - [ ] May retrieve any number in any order
  - [ ] Bonuses described on reference card

### Scoring

- [ ] Completed Concoction cards are worth 3/6/9 VPs
  - [ ] Candy on a Concoction card cannot be moved
  - [ ] Dreadful Tree candy is wild
- [ ] Harvesters score VPs = value of patch + 1 VP for each patch without a harvester in its cluster
- [ ] 1 VP for every $5 worth of candy
