# French Toast

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe
- [ ] Cooperative party game
- [ ] Objective: Guess the Secret Word within 6 rounds
- [ ] Roles: One Toastmaster knows the word; everyone else is a Guesser
- [ ] Communication: Toastmaster can only speak to repeat the current closest guess

### Flow

- [ ] Game end: 6 rounds total (or 3 rounds per team in Team Mode)
- [ ] Round structure: Toastmaster plays Hint, players discuss (optional), Toastmaster announces the current closest guess, players guess for 30s
  - [ ] Round 1 start: The "closest guess" is always "French Toast"
  - [ ] Subsequent rounds: The closest guess carries over from the previous round

### Giving Hints

- [ ] Draw 6 Hint cards and play 1 onto the Hint Scale (ranging from IS to IS NOT)
  - [ ] Discard remaining 5 cards facedown
- [ ] May move any previously played Hint cards to new positions on the scale

### Guessing

- [ ] Any player may guess at any time while the timer is running
- [ ] Guesses must express a single, complete idea (can be multiple words or proper nouns)
- [ ] Toastmaster must respond to every guess before another can be made:
  - [ ] New guess is closer: Repeat the new guess (it becomes the new "closest guess")
  - [ ] Current closest is better: Repeat the current closest guess
  - [ ] Equally close: Say "equal" (closest guess does not change)
- [ ] Correction: Toastmaster may revert the closest guess to a previous one if they change their mind
- [ ] Win condition: Correctly guess the Secret Word or a phrase containing it

### Communication Rules

- [ ] Toastmaster cannot speak except to repeat guesses, say "equal", or announce a correction
- [ ] May use tone of voice, facial expressions, and point to Hint cards
- [ ] Prohibited: Miming, gesturing to room objects, or non-verbally answering questions
