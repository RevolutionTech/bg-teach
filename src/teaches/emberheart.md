# Emberheart

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe: dragon rescue fantasy; you're champion heroes on a mystical island fighting poachers
- [ ] Objective: most Glory (VPs) after 5 rounds
- [ ] Round overview: Action → Reward → Raid → Reset

### Hirelings

- [ ] Two categories: Grunts and Experts (Scouts, Rangers, Wardens)
- [ ] Grunts go anywhere but are **discarded** after claiming a reward
- [ ] Experts return to your supply after claiming — but location-restricted:
  - [ ] Scouts: Heroes' Guild, Tavern, Poachers' Camp
  - [ ] Rangers: Garrison, Preserve
  - [ ] Wardens: Mountain only
- [ ] Spend Gear (anytime on your turn) to swap Hirelings in your personal supply — not ones already on the board

### Action Phase

- [ ] Players alternate turns; each turn = **one** of three actions:
  1. Send a Party to a Common Location
  2. Use a Special Location
  3. Take an Aide
- [ ] Phase ends when last Aide is taken (finish current round)

#### Send Party to Common Location

- [ ] Stack 1–5 Hirelings + your Crest on top → place on empty numbered space matching stack size
- [ ] Multiple Parties allowed at same location; no two on same numbered space
- [ ] Don't claim cards yet — that's the Reward phase

#### Use Special Locations

- [ ] No Hirelings, just a Crest
- [ ] **Warehouse**: +2 Gear (no Flame cost; unlimited uses)
- [ ] **Firestation**: +4 Grunts but +1 Flame (unlimited uses per round)

#### Aides (4 tokens, each once per round; no Crest needed)

- [ ] **Leader**: first player next round; may recall one Party and immediately re-place it (Companion recalled can't be re-placed this round)
- [ ] **Defender**: discard one face-up Raid card
- [ ] **Survivalist**: gain 1 Gear; breaks ties at the Mountain
- [ ] **Healer**: −2 Flame

#### Dragon Companion

- [ ] Each player has a Companion meeple; place it on your Crest atop a Party stack
- [ ] Once per round; stays until end of Reward phase, then returns
- [ ] Three Attribute tracks on your player board: Strength / Senses / Speed
  - [ ] Odd steps (1, 3, 5) = placement effects; even steps (2, 4, 6, 7) = end-game Glory
  - [ ] Use the top unlocked effect at or below your marker; all three can combine
- [ ] Strength: Companion counts as extra Hirelings (can replace all if strong enough)
- [ ] Senses: placed on space 3 → gain Gear tokens
- [ ] Speed: first to arrive at a location (no other Parties there) → decrease Flame
- [ ] Cannot send Companion to Special Locations

### Reward Phase

- [ ] Locations resolve in fixed clockwise order: Heroes' Guild → Tavern → Poachers' Camp → Mountain → Preserve → Garrison
- [ ] At each location: larger Party = earlier pick; descending order of space number
- [ ] If no card left for you (or you pass): recall entire Party + gain 2 Gear
- [ ] After each location: discard all Grunts; return Experts/Crests/Companions to players

#### Heroes' Guild (Grunt / Scout)

- [ ] Claim a Hero card; place in your area
- [ ] Hero Ability + Scoring Condition only active if a matching-color Dragon is attached
- [ ] One Dragon per Hero; once attached, permanent

#### Tavern (Grunt / Scout)

- [ ] Claim a card: gain specified Hirelings + adjust Flame as shown; then discard card

#### Poachers' Camp (Grunt / Scout)

- [ ] Claim a Captive Dragon card (weaker dragons, lower Glory)
- [ ] Exotic Dragon = counts as any color until attached to a Hero/Preserve; then adopts that color permanently
- [ ] May attach immediately or later; once attached, permanent

#### Mountain (Grunt / Warden) — 3 levels

- [ ] **Bottom level**: claim Dragon cards in Party-size order; Grunts discarded, Companions recalled; Wardens move to Warden spaces (descending Party size; Survivalist breaks ties)
- [ ] **Middle level**: claim cards; each claimed card → discard 1 additional Warden; parties that didn't claim retrieve
- [ ] **Top level**: one Dragon available; claimer also advances 1 Attribute step + discards 1 Warden + +1 Flame; Survivalist breaks ties
- [ ] Can't ascend beyond bottom without Wardens; can bail out at any level (no Gear if you already claimed)
- [ ] Dragon cards may require Warden discard or Flame increase — check before claiming
- [ ] Lose last Warden when the board requires discarding one → take +1 Flame instead
- [ ] Claimed Mountain Dragons may attach to Hero/Preserve immediately or later

#### Preserve (Grunt / Ranger)

- [ ] Claim a Preserve card; attach matching-color Dragon anytime → advance Attribute marker **2 steps** on matching track
- [ ] One Dragon per Preserve; once attached, permanent

#### Garrison (Grunt / Ranger)

- [ ] Garrison cards sorted by color into 3 decks (red/blue/green), ascending Glory; one per color available per round
- [ ] Claim regardless of whether you meet requirements; complete later by owning required items
  - [ ] Dragons: all yours count (attached or not)
  - [ ] Heroes: all yours count (attached or not)
  - [ ] Hirelings: all in supply + on board
- [ ] When complete: advance 1 Attribute step per card → tuck under player board (Glory visible)

### Raid Phase

- [ ] After Reward phase: all players discard Hirelings per the remaining Raid card on the board
- [ ] Can't discard enough? → +1 Flame per missing Hireling
- [ ] Must discard right type if you have it; raid card specifies type and trigger (per Dragon, per Garrison card, per Attribute step, etc.)

### End Game Scoring

1. Glory value of each Dragon (attached or not)
2. Highest unlocked Glory on each Attribute track (tracks don't stack)
3. Hero card Glory if matching Dragon attached **and** scoring condition met
4. Completed Garrison card Glory
5. Flame track bonus (most Flame = +0; all others get +Glory per step gap, per chart on board)
   - [ ] Before scoring Flame: may discard Gear to reduce Flame (−1 per 3 Gear discarded)

- [ ] TB: most Dragon cards

### Setup

- [ ] Place Attribute markers: one on each of your 3 tracks, then move **one marker** of your choice up 1 step
  - [ ] First game: recommended to move Strength (leftmost)
