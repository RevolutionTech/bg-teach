# Let's Go! To Japan

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe
- [ ] Drafting and tableau building
- [ ] Objective: most victory points (VP)
- [ ] Game end: after 13 rounds of planning, players resolve their 6-day trip

### Planning Phase (Rounds 1-13)

- [ ] Round overview: Draw cards, Play cards, Resolve Day Bonuses (if 3rd card placed), Pass cards
  - [ ] Passing direction: rounds 1-7 Left, rounds 8-12 Right
- [ ] Round 13: draw 1 card, keep both cards (no passing)
- [ ] Research tokens: discard to draw 3 cards (any mix), then discard 3 cards from hand
  - [ ] Use any number of times during turn

### Card Placement & Actions

- [ ] Place card in a column (Mon–Sat)
  - [ ] Max 3 cards per day (unless "Extra Walk" used)
  - [ ] Cards never move once placed
- [ ] Card position:
  - [ ] Bottom card: "Highlight of the Day" (only this card's bonus can be scored)
  - [ ] Tucked cards: only top icons/symbols matter
- [ ] Go for a Walk: discard 1 card from hand to play top card of Tokyo/Kyoto deck face-down
  - [ ] Gain 1 Research token immediately
  - [ ] Face-down cards count toward day limit

### Day Bonuses

- [ ] Triggered when placing 3rd card in a day
- [ ] Count icons matching that day's Favorable Condition token
- [ ] Rewards:
  - [ ] 1 Match: +1 Mood OR 2 Research tokens
  - [ ] 2 Matches: 1 Wild token OR any "1 Match" reward
  - [ ] 3+ Matches: 1 Luxury Train OR "Extra Walk" (4th card) OR any lower reward
- [ ] Mood Track: if token hits far right, +1 Happiness (reset to center); if far left, +1 Stress (reset to center)

### The Trip (Final Round)

- [ ] Resolve itinerary from Monday to Saturday, top card to bottom card
- [ ] Experience: move tokens on 5 Experience tracks based on card icons
- [ ] Trains: if card city differs from previous card city, place a Train token between them
  - [ ] Regular Train (from supply): -2 VP
  - [ ] Luxury Train (from Day Bonus): +2 VP, +1 Mood
  - [ ] Start of trip (Monday first card): free "0 VP" train
- [ ] Highlights: score Highlight of the Day bonus if requirements met
- [ ] Walks: flip face-down cards
  - [ ] Spend Walk token: flip face-up, resolve icons/points
  - [ ] No Walk token: stay face-down (+1 VP, +1 Mood, +1 Walk icon)

### Scoring

- [ ] VPs on cards + Highlight bonuses
- [ ] Happiness vs. Stress track: net VPs based on final positions
- [ ] Experience Tracks: VPs based on progress in each of 5 categories
- [ ] Train penalties/bonuses
- [ ] Unspent Research: 1 VP each
- [ ] Tiebreaker: total steps on all Experience tracks
