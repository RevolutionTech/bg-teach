# Sail

### Overview
- [ ] Theme & vibe: 2-player cooperative nautical trick-taker
- [ ] Objective: sail the ship onto the End Token
- [ ] Game end: win immediately on reaching End Token; lose if any losing condition triggers

### Deal & Exchange
- [ ] Each round, deal 9 cards each from the Player Deck
- [ ] Each player secretly chooses one card to pass to the other — must choose **before** seeing the card you receive
- [ ] No communication from deal until after the final trick of the round

### Playing Tricks
- [ ] Player with the Round Disc on their side leads the first trick; trick winner leads the next
- [ ] Second player must follow the lead color if able
- [ ] Highest card **matching the lead color** wins the trick; advance that player's Trick Cube

### Actions
- [ ] Every trick triggers an action based on the **symbol pair** on the two cards played
- [ ] Two helms: move ship forward & toward trick winner
  - [ ] Kraken damages ship once per Kraken symbol
- [ ] Helm/Kraken + Cannon: Deal damage to Kraken (bury the Helm/Kraken card in Kraken Deck)
- [ ] Two mermaids: move ship straight forward one space
- [ ] Two cannons: flip top card of Player Deck and resolve its Cannon Action; discard it
- [ ] Any other combo: no action; advance Trick Cube only

### Kraken Damage
- [ ] Each damage = discard top card of Kraken Deck
- [ ] If the Kraken **Card** would be discarded: instead move Kraken Meeple one space clockwise, bury Kraken Card at bottom of deck
- [ ] Lose immediately if the Kraken Card is the **only** card left in the Kraken Deck

### Round End
- [ ] Round ends when one player wins their **4th trick**
- [ ] Kraken damages ship a number of times = number shown under Kraken Meeple
- [ ] Advance Round Tracker; reset Trick Cubes to 0
- [ ] Shuffle Player Deck + discard + leftover hand cards together for the next round
- [ ] Game is played over **5 rounds** max

### Sailing Hazards
- [ ] **Islands**: blocks movement
- [ ] **Kraken spaces**: entering one deals Kraken damage
- [ ] **Edge of board**: ship cannot move off

### Losing Conditions
- [ ] Round 5 ends
- [ ] End of Round 2: ship not fully past the **first storm**
- [ ] End of Round 4: ship not fully past the **second storm**
- [ ] Kraken Card is the only card left in Kraken Deck
- [ ] Kraken Meeple reaches the **'Dead'** space
- [ ] If an action causes both win and lose simultaneously → **you win**

### Setup
- [ ] Each player chooses a **Pirate Card** (special ability)
