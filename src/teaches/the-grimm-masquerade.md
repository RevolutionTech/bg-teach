# The Grimm Masquerade

### Overview
- [ ] Theme & vibe: secret fairy-tale character at a masquerade ball
- [ ] Social deduction
- [ ] Goal: most Roses
- [ ] Game End: 3 rounds or 10 Roses

### Secret Identity
- [ ] Each player has a new secret Character card each round
- [ ] Character has a **Boon** (top-left) and **Bane** (bottom-right) artifact type

### Artifacts
- [ ] All artifacts in your tableau are face-up
- [ ] Collect 3 Boon → win the round immediately
- [ ] Collect 2 Bane → **unmasked** (identity revealed)
- [ ] 6 copies of each artifact type in the game

### Round
- [ ] Win a round: collect 3 of your Boon artifact OR last one still masked
- [ ] Rose Trophy: worth 1/3/5 Roses (rounds 1/2/3)

### Turn
1. **Distribute Artifacts**
   - Draw 1st: decide to keep or give away *before* drawing the 2nd
   - Draw 2nd: must do the *opposite* of your 1st choice
2. **Action (Optional)**
   — Discard a matching pair from your tableau to use an action

### Being Unmasked
- [ ] Immediately: reveal identity; cover Character on board with Broken Mirror; take all your artifacts into hand
- [ ] Player who gave you the 2nd Bane gains a Rose
  - [ ] If you drew your own Bane and were forced to keep it, no Rose is awarded
- [ ] On your turn (unmasked): draw 1 artifact, add to hand; give 1 from hand to any still-masked player
- [ ] Cannot win round, but can still gain Roses by unmasking others

### Evidence Markers
- [ ] Tracks who you are *not*
- [ ] Required whenever: 2 matching artifacts (not your Bane), 3 matching artifacts (not your Boon), some actions
  - [ ] Marker stays even if you later discard the relevant artifacts
- [ ] Never place evidence markers on a Character already marked with your color, or one already unmasked

### Actions
- [ ] **Point the Finger**: guess another player's Character
  - [ ] Correct: they're unmasked; you gain 2 Roses (1 for unmasking + 1 for correct guess)
  - [ ] Incorrect: accused gains 1 Rose; they place Evidence marker on the guessed Character
- [ ] Two random actions revealed each round
- [ ] Artifact exchanges are simultaneous — no info revealed mid-exchange
- [ ] A single action can unmask multiple players; gain 1 Rose per player unmasked

### Round Reset
- [ ] Player with fewest Roses = new First Player (TB: clockwise from player with most Roses)
- [ ] Roses kept, but everything else reset

### Wagers
- [ ] One Wager card per Character; held face-down
- [ ] Gain a Wager when unmasked on your turn (max 1 per round) or by spending a pair on **Secrets** action (no round limit)
- [ ] End of round: if your Wager matches the round winner → gain Roses = Rose Trophy value

### Scoring
- [ ] Most Roses wins
- [ ] TB: most Roses from Rose Trophies

### Setup
- [ ] Draft 1 artifact from the face-up cards (in turn order)
