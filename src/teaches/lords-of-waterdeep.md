# Lords of Waterdeep

### Overview
- [ ] Theme: Secretly rule the City of Splendors by hiring adventurers to complete quests
- [ ] Objective: Most victory points (VP)
- [ ] Game end: 8 rounds

### Flow
- [ ] Round structure: Replenish VP on buildings, start-of-round effects, take turns, reassign Harbor agents
- [ ] Turn structure: assign agent, complete quest
- [ ] Round 5: Everyone adds +1 Agent to pool for remainder of game

### Actions
- [ ] Assign Agent: Place 1 Agent on unoccupied action space; resolve immediately
  - [ ] Cannot skip turn if Agents are available in pool
- [ ] Builder's Hall: Purchase 1 face-up building tile
  - [ ] Pay gold, score printed VP, place ownership marker
  - [ ] Building remains in play; owner gets "Owner Benefit" if others use it
- [ ] Waterdeep Harbor: Play 1 Intrigue card from hand
  - [ ] Reassignment: After all Agents are assigned, Harbor Agents move to any open spaces in numerical order
- [ ] Cliffwatch Inn: Acquire new Quest cards
  - [ ] Spaces provide: +2 Gold OR +1 Intrigue card OR reset quest row then take 1
- [ ] Castle Waterdeep: Take First Player marker and 1 Intrigue card
- [ ] Resource Buildings: Gain Clerics (white), Fighters (orange), Rogues (black), Wizards (purple), or Gold

### Quests
- [ ] Complete Quest: Spend required Adventurers/Gold from Tavern; gain reward and VP
  - [ ] Max 1 quest completed per turn
- [ ] Plot Quests: Provide ongoing/permanent benefits once completed
- [ ] Mandatory Quests: Assigned to opponents via Intrigue; recipient cannot complete other quests until finished

### Scoring
- [ ] 1 VP per Adventurer in Tavern
- [ ] 1 VP per 2 Gold in Tavern (round down)
- [ ] Lord cards
- [ ] Tiebreaker: gold
