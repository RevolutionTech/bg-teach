# Surfosaurus MAX

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe: Dinosaurs surfing the "MAX" waves
- [ ] Semi-cooperative poker
- [ ] Objective: earn most VPs by contributing to the strongest shared hands
- [ ] Game end: everyone has been the starting player 3/2/2/1/1 times

### Flow

- [ ] Round overview: Players take turns until everyone has played 3/3/2/2/2 cards
- [ ] Turn overview: Play 1 card, draw back up to 7
- [ ] End of Round:
  - [ ] Identify the single strongest combination of 4/4/4/5/5 cards from all played cards
  - [ ] Players who contributed card(s) to that combination score them
  - [ ] Excess cards or cards from losing combinations are discarded
- [ ] Do NOT discard your remaining hand cards between rounds

### Scoring & Ties

- [ ] Card Values: Higher values (up to 12) have fewer VPs; lower values (down to 1) have more VPs
- [ ] Ties: Always resolve in favor of the higher value card(s)
- [ ] Scoring Full VPs: Place cards under your "Full Coconut" card
- [ ] Scoring Half VPs: Truly tied cards (same value within the winning combo) score half VPs; place under "Half Coconut"
- [ ] Tiebreaker: most scored cards
