### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe: cozy, artisan dragons creating goods
- [ ] Worker placement and engine building
- [ ] Objective: most reputation (VP)
- [ ] Game end trigger: Artisan or Enchantment deck runs out
  - [ ] All players (including triggering player) take 1 final turn

### Turn

- [ ] Turn overview: visit a Shop, then Gather OR Enchant
  - [ ] If others present, give 1 good/coin to each occupant

### Gather

- [ ] **Collect:** gain 1 good/coin/dragon from Shop, plus 1 good from each Dragon and Enchantment there
  - [ ] Artisan icon: draw from Park or deck
- [ ] **Place Dragon** (optional): add dragon from hand to any empty matching slot; take slot reward
  - [ ] If shop filled: draw new shop (placed facedown, revealed at end of turn) (max 12/12/14/14/14)
- [ ] **Fire Up** (optional): activate 1 dragon ability at current shop
- [ ] **Shop Ability** (optional): resolve unique shop power

### Enchant

- [ ] **Cast:** choose faceup Enchantment matching Shop icon, pay goods, gain reward, and tuck
  - [ ] Max 3 enchantments per shop
  - [ ] Coins wild unless "No Coin" symbol present
- [ ] **Fire Up ALL** (optional): activate all dragon abilities at current shop

### End of Turn

- [ ] **Expand:** flip any newly drawn shops
- [ ] **Limits:** discard down to 6 dragons and 7 of each good type
  - [ ] No limit on Coins or Fancy dragons
- [ ] **Refresh:** refill Park and Enchantments to 5

### Scoring

- [ ] **Coins:** 1 VP each
- [ ] **Fancy Dragons:** reveal fulfilled goals
  - [ ] Sun: scored during turn; Moon: scored at game end
  - [ ] Cannot use Coins for Moon dragon goals
- [ ] **Tiebreaker:** most dragons in hand, most goods

### Setup

- [ ] Select 1 of 2 dealt secret Fancy dragons
- [ ] 4th/5th players: start with 1 good of choice
