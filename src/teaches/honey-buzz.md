# Honey Buzz

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe
- [ ] Worker placement, tile placement, pattern building
- [ ] Objective: most points

### Gameplay Overview

- [ ] Connect hive tiles to make empty cells for storing nectar
- [ ] Move your forage token through the field to collect nectar
- [ ] Place your fan token next to nectar tiles to produce honey
- [ ] Trade honey and pollen for points by selling to the market and completing orders
- [ ] Game end: 4/5 resources drop to min spot OR 2/3 order stacks are completed, then complete the round

### Turn

- [ ] Take a tile
  - [ ] Place workers in a stack to a hive box
  - [ ] Your stack must be taller than other stacks in the same box
  - [ ] Place the tile in your hive, must touch yellow edge to yellow edge
  - [ ] Decree tile: 5 coins
  - [ ] If you created an empty space, take hive actions
- [ ] Recall workers
  - [ ] Move all workers to player board
  - [ ] Move forage token one space (cannot spend coins for movement nor collect nectar/pollen)

### Hive Actions

- [ ] Forage: move your forage token orthogonally 1 space
  - [ ] Forage tokens do not block each other
  - [ ] Spend 2 coins per additional space 
  - [ ] Gain nectar, if space matches empty cell in hive (otherwise, gain pollen)
  - [ ] 4 possible cell patterns for 4 types of honey
- [ ] Produce: place fan on any space of hive, gain honey matching adjacent nectar
  - [ ] A nectar tile can only hold 1 honey at a time
- [ ] Market: either sell to the market or complete an order
  - [ ] Sell to the market: sell any quantity of one honey/pollen, gain coins, drop value by 1
  - [ ] Complete order: return resources and take card, gain the action from the column anytime that turn
    - [ ] At end of turn, reveal the next order
- [ ] New Bee: add worker from supply to nursery
- [ ] Accounting: gain 5 coins
- [ ] Decree: wild action

### Scoring

- [ ] Queen's Contests: payout to top N-1 players
  - [ ] Speed: offer coins upon completion
  - [ ] Final: offer points at end of game
- [ ] 1 coin = 1 honey = 1 pollen = 1 point
- [ ] Orders
- [ ] Tiebreaker: most remaining resources, most completed orders
