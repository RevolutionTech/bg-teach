# Phantom Ink: Arcana

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe
- [ ] Team-based party game
- [ ] Objective: guess the secret Object before the other team
- [ ] One Spirit on each team knows the secret Object, other players are Mediums
- [ ] Spirits can only communicate by answering questions, one letter at a time

### Turn

- [ ] Turn overview: play arcana (optional), then ask a question or make a guess, draw to hand limit
- [ ] Ask
  - [ ] Say "Silencio" to tell the Spirit to stop writing
- [ ] Guess
- [ ] Eye space: ask the other team to add another letter before your turn
- [ ] Play arcana upside-down to discard questions

### Clues

- [ ] When a clue/guess is complete, the last letter will be a period
- [ ] Clues may contain spaces, but not numbers or special characters
- [ ] Clues may not contain any form of the Object
