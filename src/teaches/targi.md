# Targi

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe
- [ ] 2-player worker placement and grid movement game
- [ ] Objective: most victory points (VP)
- [ ] Game end: a player completes 12-card tribe display OR robber completes full circuit (after 4th raid)

### Flow

- [ ] Round overview: move robber, place Targi figures, place tribe markers, resolve actions, reset center
- [ ] Phase 1: Advance Robber
  - [ ] Move clockwise to next border card
  - [ ] If robber reaches a corner, resolve Raid immediately (lose goods/VP/gold)
- [ ] Phase 2: Place Targi Figures
  - [ ] Take turns placing 3 Targi figures on border cards
  - [ ] Cannot place on: robber's card, Raid corners, cards occupied by any Targi, or cards directly opposite opponent's Targi
- [ ] Phase 3: Place Tribe Markers
  - [ ] Identify intersections of row/column lines from your Targis
  - [ ] Place markers on empty center cards at these intersections (usually 2, sometimes 1)
- [ ] Phase 4: Resolve Actions
  - [ ] Start player resolves all their occupied cards (border and center) in any order, then 2nd player
  - [ ] Retrieve Targi/marker as each action is completed
  - [ ] Center cards are replaced with opposite type (Goods -> Tribe, Tribe -> Goods) face down
- [ ] Phase 5: Cleanup
  - [ ] Turn all face-down center cards face-up
  - [ ] Pass start player token

### Actions & Cards

- [ ] Goods cards: take indicated resources (Dates, Salt, Pepper, Gold, or VP)
- [ ] Tribe cards: pay cost to add to 3x4 display
  - [ ] May hold 1 tribe card in hand; cannot swap or discard unless using Noble action
  - [ ] Display from left to right in any of your 3 rows
  - [ ] Provides immediate or permanent advantages and end-game VP
- [ ] Border cards: resolve unique effects (e.g., Noble to play card from hand, Trader to exchange goods)
- [ ] Hand limit: max 1 tribe card
- [ ] Resource limit (end of round): max 10 goods tokens and 3 gold tokens

### Scoring

- [ ] VP tokens collected during play
- [ ] VP printed on displayed tribe cards
- [ ] Row bonuses:
  - [ ] +4 VP: 4 cards with identical symbols
  - [ ] +2 VP: 4 cards with unique symbols
- [ ] Special scoring from tribe card abilities
- [ ] Tiebreakers: most gold, then most goods
