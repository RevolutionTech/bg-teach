# Disney Villainous

### Overview
- [ ] Theme & vibe: asymmetric Disney villain action selection
- [ ] Objective: be first to complete YOUR specific objective
- [ ] Game end: as soon as one player fulfills their objective, they win immediately
  - [ ] Some objectives only check at the START of your turn (eg. Jafar, Maleficent, Prince John, Ursula)

### Your Board (Realm)
- [ ] Each board has 4 locations; your villain mover starts at the leftmost
- [ ] Bottom of board = your Villain cards; top of board = Fate cards played by opponents
- [ ] All cards on your board = your "Realm"; card abilities only affect cards in the same Realm
- [ ] Some locations start locked — place a Lock Token there; you cannot move to, play to, or move from a locked location

### Turn
1. **Move your Villain**: must move to a different location (cannot stay)
2. **Perform Actions**: do available actions at your new location in any order
  - [ ] Each action usable once per symbol shown
  - [ ] All optional
3. **Draw Cards**: refill hand to 4 from your Villain deck at end of turn
  - [ ] If deck is empty, shuffle discard

### Actions
- [ ] **Gain Power**: take that many Power tokens from the Cauldron (Power = currency to play cards)
- [ ] **Play a Card**: play one card from hand, pay its Cost; Items/Allies go below location (bottom); one card per symbol
- [ ] **Discard Cards**: discard any number of hand cards face-up; draw back up at end of turn (not immediately)
- [ ] **Move an Item or Ally**: move one to an adjacent unlocked location; cannot move items attached to Allies/Heroes
- [ ] **Move a Hero**: move one Hero in your Realm to an adjacent location; cannot move into/out of locked location
- [ ] **Vanquish**: defeat a Hero using Allies at the SAME location; combined Ally Strength must ≥ Hero Strength; discard used Allies to Villain discard, Hero to Fate discard
  - [ ] A Hero's strength is its printed value modified by any active card abilities
  - [ ] If strength reduced to 0, still need a Vanquish action (but no Allies need to be discarded)
- [ ] **Activate**: trigger an Activate-symbol card's ability; pay its Activation Cost if any; card stays in Realm (no limit on uses)
- [ ] **Fate**: reveal top 2 cards of a chosen opponent's Fate deck; play one against them, discard the other
  - [ ] Heroes are played to the TOP of their board, covering that location's action symbols (blocking those actions)
  - [ ] If a Fate card can't legally be played, discard it; if neither can be played, discard both
  - [ ] 5–6 player games: targeted player takes the Fate Token and cannot be targeted again until someone else is

### Card Types
- [ ] **Ally** (Villain deck) — henchmen; play to any unlocked location; use to Vanquish or keep for ongoing ability
- [ ] **Hero** (Fate deck) — played to TOP of opponent's board, covering action symbols; most have abilities that hinder that villain
  - [ ] Multiple Heroes stack at a location; only topmost one covers symbols
  - [ ] When topmost Hero is defeated, slide remaining Heroes down to cover symbols
- [ ] **Item** (both decks) — ongoing ability; Villain Items play to bottom of board; Fate Items attach to a Hero
  - [ ] Items attached to a Hero/Ally move and discard with them
- [ ] **Effect** (both decks) — one-time; pay Cost, resolve, discard immediately
- [ ] **Condition** (Villain deck) — played during an OPPONENT'S turn when its trigger condition is met; no Play a Card action needed; do not draw a replacement until end of your own turn
