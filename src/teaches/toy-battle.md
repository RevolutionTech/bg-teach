# Toy Battle

### Overview

- [ ] Two-player tactical skirmish
- [ ] Game end: Instant win upon objective, or when a player cannot draw/place
- [ ] Objective: Capture enemy H.Q. or reach Medal objective
  - [ ] If deck/placement stalls: player with most Medals wins
  - [ ] Tiebreaker: player who ended the game loses

### Flow

- [ ] Turn overview: Perform one of two actions (Draw 2 or Place 1)

### Actions

- [ ] **Draw 2 Troops**
  - [ ] From personal reserve to rack
  - [ ] Hand limit: 8 Troops maximum
  - [ ] Can draw 1 if only 1 remains in reserve or only 1 rack space left
- [ ] **Place 1 Troop**
  - [ ] Must be connected to your H.Q. by a continuous path of your own Troops
  - [ ] Place on: Empty base, your own Troop, or weaker enemy Troop (strictly lower strength)
  - [ ] Capture H.Q.: Place on enemy H.Q. to win immediately (cannot place on your own)
  - [ ] Resolve: Apply Troop effect, then special base effect (if using advanced rules)

### Placement Rules

- [ ] **Stacking**: No limit to stack height; only the top Troop occupies the base
  - [ ] May inspect stacks, but never change order
- [ ] **Connections**: Empty bases or enemy-occupied bases cut the connection to your H.Q.

### Regions &amp; Medals

- [ ] **Control**: Automatically take control when you occupy all bases surrounding a region
- [ ] **Medals**: Take all Medals from the center of the region immediately
  - [ ] Medals are kept even if you later lose control of the region
- [ ] **Multiple Regions**: Can trigger and take control of multiple regions in one turn
