# The Shipwreck Arcana

### Overview
- [ ] Cooperative deduction game
- [ ] Theme & vibe: fortune tellers in a drowned world predicting each other's fates
- [ ] Win: 7 correct predictions (green tracker reaches 7)
- [ ] Lose: doom reaches 7 (red tracker)

### Key Concepts
- [ ] Each turn, the active player draws 2 fate tokens (values 1–7) and keeps them hidden
- [ ] They play 1 fate face-up onto an arcana card, obeying that card's rule
- [ ] The other players try to deduce the value of the unplayed fate
- [ ] Where the active player plays (and where they *don't*) is how information is communicated
- [ ] Active player must not talk or give nonverbal hints during their turn

### Turn Order
1. **Draw**: draw from bag until you have 2 fates; if you kept one from last turn, draw only 1
2. **Play**: play 1 fate face-up onto a card in the central area, obeying that card's ability
   - [ ] If multiple cards can be obeyed, you choose which one to obey
   - [ ] If holding a kept fate, give no indication which fate you're playing
3. **Predict** (optional): allies discuss and may make a single unanimous prediction: "We predict your fate is ___"
   - [ ] Must be a single value; group gets one prediction
   - [ ] Allies may flip your number tokens to record what they think they know
   - [ ] If no prediction is made, keep fate face-down and don't reset number line
4. **Resolve**: correct prediction: +1 score; incorrect: +1 doom; either way, discard fate and reset number line
5. **Fade**: check if any arcana cards fade

### Arcana Cards & Fading
- [ ] 5 cards always in the central area: *The Hours* (special, never fades) + 4 arcana cards
- [ ] Fates played onto *The Hours* slide to the arcana card immediately to its right
- [ ] Each arcana card has a duration (moon icons); when the total hours (pip icons) of fates on it ≥ duration, it fades
  - [ ] Fates with value 1–3: 1 hour pip; 4–6: 2 pips; 7: 3 pips
- [ ] When a card fades: discard its fates, flip it below the play area, +2 doom (unless a correct prediction was made this turn), refill from deck

### Faded Powers
- [ ] The back of each faded arcana card has a one-use power
- [ ] After using, send card to bottom of deck
- [ ] Only 1 faded power per turn; using one doesn't force a prediction
- [ ] Some powers are active-player only; some are group only

### Doom Stacking
- [ ] Incorrect prediction (+1) and a card fading (+2) in the same turn **both** apply
- [ ] Multiple cards fading = +2 each
