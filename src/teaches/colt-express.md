# Colt Express

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe
- [ ] Objective: most money
  - [ ] Most shots: $1000
- [ ] Game end: 5 rounds
- [ ] Round overview: play cards, resolve cards

### Round

- [ ] Round card determines num of turns in the round
- [ ] Each turn either: play a card face-up to the common deck, or draw 3 cards into hand
- [ ] End of phase: return all cards in hand to deck
- [ ] Flip common deck over and resolve all action cards in order
  - [ ] Actions are obligatory (if still possible)
- [ ] Shuffle all player cards and bullet cards hit with, then first player passes

### Actions

- [ ] Move: adjacent car (if inside) or 1-3 cars (if on roof)
- [ ] Floor Change
- [ ] Marshal: adjacent car, always inside
  - [ ] Bandits cannot be in the same car as the Marshal, they take a bullet and escape to the roof
- [ ] Fire: bandit in adjacent car (if inside) or anyone in LOS (if on roof)
  - [ ] If valid target, give that player your next bullet
  - [ ] Can never shoot at someone in the same car
- [ ] Robbery: take a loot token
- [ ] Punch: target a bandit in same car, they drop a loot token and move to an adjacent car

### Miscellaneous

- [ ] Bandits can never leave the train
- [ ] The round card may require cards to be placed face-down or have other events
