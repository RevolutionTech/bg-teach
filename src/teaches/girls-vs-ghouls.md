# Girls vs Ghouls

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe
- [ ] Social deduction
- [ ] Objective: convince trick-or-treaters to come to the houses belonging to your team to earn points for your team and win the game
- [ ] Two teams: girls, ghouls
  - [ ] (Odd player counts only) Reporter wants the teams to tie

### Round

- [ ] 3 rounds
  - [ ] House with most good candy gets point card, house with most black candy gets flipped to opposite team
- [ ] Round overview: draw hand of 5 cards, players take turns clockwise playing 1 card per turn until all cards have been played, resolve tricks, score round, remove all cubes, pass first player token
- [ ] Turn: move 0-2 spaces, 1 action
- [ ] Actions: treat, trick, peek, sneak
- [ ] Treat: play treat faceup and add cubes
- [ ] Trick: play trick facedown on house
  - [ ] Trick effects: flip house, invert value, double value, cancel trick
  - [ ] Tricks resolved in order played
- [ ] Peek: discard 1 card to view all tricks at house
- [ ] Sneak: discard 1 card to move anywhere
