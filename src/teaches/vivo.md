# Vivo

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe: musical puppy performance
- [ ] Trick-taking
- [ ] Objective: most points after 2 rounds (12 tricks per round)
- [ ] Every trick has a "Harmony" card that dictates how many suits must be present

### Following the Harmony

- [ ] Check the Harmony card (Solo/1, Duet/2, Trio/3, or Quartet/4)
- [ ] If the required number of suits are already in the trick: must play a previously played suit
- [ ] If required suits are NOT yet met:
  - [ ] May play any suit, provided the requirement can still be met by the remaining players
  - [ ] If you are the last player(s) and the requirement isn't met: **must** play a new suit
- [ ] Playing Off-Harmony:
  - [ ] If you cannot follow the rules above, play any card faceup
  - [ ] Off-harmony cards are ignored for scoring (cannot be high/low)

### Scoring a Trick

- [ ] Highest Rank: flip card facedown and add to scoring row (worth 2 points)
- [ ] Lowest Rank: keep card faceup and add to scoring row (worth card's **rank** in points)
- [ ] Discard all other cards
- [ ] Ties: if tied for high or low, **later** in the trick wins
  - [ ] If all players tie, the tie-winner scores "Highest" (facedown); no "Lowest" is scored

### Next Trick & Round End

- [ ] Replace Harmony card with the next one from the deck
- [ ] Lead: player who scored **Lowest** rank leads next
  - [ ] If no one scored lowest, **Highest** scores leads
