# Libertalia: Winds of Galecrest

### Overview

- [ ] Theme & vibe
- [ ] Objective: most doubloons after 3 voyages
- [ ] Day overview: play character card simultaneously, activate characters abilities and steal loot in order
- [ ] Voyage overview: gain 6 cards, play 4-6 days, score loot

### Day

- [ ] Choose character simultaneously
- [ ] Arrange characters by rank
  - [ ] Tiebreaker: player reputation
- [ ] Daytime abilities (from left to right)
- [ ] Dusk (from right to left)
  - [ ] Gain loot token
  - [ ] Dusk abilities
  - [ ] Move character to ship
- [ ] Loot tokens not taken are returned to the bag
- [ ] Night abilities (simultaneous, all characters in ship)

### Voyage

- [ ] Start of voyage
  - [ ] All players gain 6 identical characters
  - [ ] Gain doubloons according to reputation
- [ ] # days: 4, 5, 6
- [ ] End of voyage
  - [ ] Anchor abilities (simultaneous, all characters in ship)
  - [ ] Bury doubloons (safe in future voyages)
  - [ ] Return all loot tokens to bag, all characters to graveyard

### Miscellaneous

- [ ] When choosing order of operations, cannot chose order that avoids penalties
